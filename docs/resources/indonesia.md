## Emergency Numbers / Nomor Darurat

**National Emergency Numbers:**
- **112** - National Emergency Number (Nomor Darurat Nasional)
- **118** - Ambulance (Ambulans)
- **119** - Fire Department (Pemadam Kebakaran)
- **110** - Police (Polisi)
- **115** - Search and Rescue (SAR)
- **129** - Poisons Information Center
