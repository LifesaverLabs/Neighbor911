## Emergency Numbers

**National Emergency Numbers:**
- **112** - National Emergency Number (All emergencies)
- **108** - Emergency Ambulance Service (National Ambulance Service)
- **102** - Ambulance Service (Some states)
- **100** - Police
- **101** - Fire Brigade
