## Emergency Numbers

**National Emergency Numbers:**
- **1122** - Emergency Services (Rescue 1122 in Punjab, KP)
- **115** - Edhi Emergency Ambulance Service
- **15** - Police
- **16** - Fire Brigade
- **1299** - Chhipa Ambulance (Karachi)
